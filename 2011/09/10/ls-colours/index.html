ls colours

10 Sep, 2011 · 22 Words
shell

To get ls colours on a mac add the follow to your .bash_profile.

#light colours
export LSCOLORS=ExFxCxDxbxegedabagacad

# dark colours
export LSCOLORS=gxBxhxDxfxhxhxhxhxcxcx
